The Challenge

Automation in software engineering increases coordination costs for teams, slowing down market adaptation. The coming wave of agentic AI demands shared, explicit, domain-specific models to support reliable decision-making.

Our Approach

We’re catching the wave by balancing the relational complexity carried by individuals and building an orchestration engine where cooperative games between teams form effortlessly.

We amplify human intelligence, not replace it.

OBSERVE

Things that happen

Events are things that happen once. We try to understand them from different perspectives, such as user experience or infrastructure.

Understanding reliability is everyone's job.

LEARN

Sense of activity

We care about the conditions in which relevant events happen. They help us form a sense of context and give us the ability to grasp what’s going on.

Causal understanding enables the best possible decisions.

CAPABILITY

Power to run

We build capabilities with the power to run activity the way it needs to be: with all the data required to meet user needs, and with trusted, correct data available at the right time.

Cost of delay is real — in architecture, it’s often hidden.

ASSIST

Flow solves autonomy

We do capability design for Flow — focusing on enabling change in a predictable ways with an affordable rate. Capability design can be seen as the ‘theory’ or shared model that best explains what is happening.

Decision quality improves by designing systems that let the right decisions happen.

COHERENCE

Engine for Flow

When we don’t design for Flow, shared models become vulnerable to erosion. People responsible for maintaining coherence are often busy and overwhelmed.

Designed for teams’ collective agency.
